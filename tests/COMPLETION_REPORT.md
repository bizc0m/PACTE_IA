## Innovation & Differentiation

This testing suite is not a survey or focus group. It's a **behavioral test** that:

1. **Observes real interactions** — Not asking opinions, watching behavior
2. **Measures objectively** — Scores 0-100, not just "yes/no"
3. **Grounds in reality** — 5 realistic scenarios, not hypotheticals
4. **Respects ethics** — Aligned with PACTE_IA framework
5. **Enables decision-making** — Clear go/no-go criteria
6. **Documents rigorously** — Every interaction recorded and scored
7. **Plans for iteration** — Built-in re-test capability if needed
