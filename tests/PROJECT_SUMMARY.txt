SUCCESS METRICS SUMMARY
================================================================================

Clarity Score (0-100)
  Threshold: ≥70%
  Measures: User understands the diagnosis
  Questions: 5 specific questions about diagnosis comprehension
  Formula: Rubric-based with adjustment factors

Autonomy Score (0-100) ★ CRITICAL
  Threshold: ≥70%
  Measures: User can actually choose, not forced
  Questions: 5 specific questions about freedom of choice
  Formula: Rubric-based with adjustment factors
  Importance: Core to PACTE_IA (human agency)

Effectiveness Score (0-100)
  Threshold: ≥70%
  Measures: Did Reset actually solve the problem?
  Questions: 5 specific questions about resolution
  Formula: Rubric-based with adjustment factors

Efficiency Score (0-100)
  Threshold: ≤2 cycles (= Efficiency ≥75%)
  Measures: How many iterations to resolve?
  Calculation: (cycles - 1) × 20 points deducted
  Formula: 100 - (20 × (cycles - 1))

Trust Score (0-100)
  Threshold: ≥70% (lower threshold, bonus metric)
  Measures: Would user recommend/re-use this IA?
  Questions: 5 specific questions about trust and advocacy
  Formula: Sentiment analysis + recommendation score

Dignity Score (Pass/Fail) ★ NON-NEGOTIABLE
  Threshold: PASS on 100% of sessions (mandatory)
  Measures: User felt respected, not manipulated
  Violations: Apology script, manipulation, coercion, gaslighting
  Formula: Any violation = AUTOMATIC FAIL
  Importance: Ethical requirement, PACTE_IA core

OVERALL SUCCESS CRITERIA:
  All 6 metrics must meet or exceed thresholds
  If any fails: Cannot deploy without remediation
  Decision rule: ≥5/6 pass = iterate + re-test, <4/6 pass = redesign
